How We Assess — The BSI Assessment Standard

"Structured Authentic Assessment" — rigorous like a standardised test, natural like play.

1
Prepare
Set up materials per item
2
Warm-up
Build rapport, ease anxiety
3
1:1 Assessment
Authentic tasks, observe process
4
Score 0–2
2=independent · 1=with help · 0=not yet
5
Observe & Record
Behaviour/affect observation layer + evidence
📌 Scoring is developmental, not comparative: results describe what a child can do and the next step to grow — never a public ranking. Social-emotional growth is captured through an observation layer alongside the scored tasks.
